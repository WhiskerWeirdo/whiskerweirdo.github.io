Interactive Engagement

With real-time responsiveness to channel events, BanterBrain companions react to viewers gifts, cheers and commands. They can provide an interactive experience that keeps your audience engaged and entertained. Configured how you as the streamer prefer this companion to act.

Personalized Experiences

Each AI companion can be tailored to reflect your brand and streaming style. Whether you need a witty commentator, a knowledgeable expert, or a friendly conversationalist, your AI companion adapts to fit the vibe of your channel. This personality can be matched by your preferred natural voice.

Text-to-Speech Services

Transform written content into natural-sounding speech with our range of Text-to-Speech (TTS) services. Choose from:

  • Azure TTS: Access a wide range of voices and languages with Azure's cloud-based TTS service.
  • ElevenLabs: Experience high-quality, lifelike voices powered by ElevenLabs' advanced TTS technology.
